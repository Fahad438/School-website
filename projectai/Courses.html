Biology

Biology is defined as the science of life or biology, and it is the science that searches for the characteristics that distinguish humans, animals, and plants from inanimate objects, such as nutrition, growth, reproduction, etc. [1] The concept of biology (in English: Biology) was derived from two Greek words: (bios); It means life, and (logos); It means science or study, and it is a science that studies the cellular structure of all life forms. Simple ones, such as bacteria that consist of one cell, and the complex that consists of several cells. It is also concerned with studying the classification of living organisms, their behavior, and their evolution.
